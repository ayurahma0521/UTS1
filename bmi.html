ABOUT BMI


Body Mass index (BMI) is a numerical value that helps asses whether an individual's weight is within a healthy range based on their height. It's a simple calculation that provides insight into wheter someone is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

Here are the BMI Categories for both men and women :


  • Below 18.5: Underweight
  • 18.5 to 29.9 : Normal Weight
  • 25.0 to 29.9 : Overweight
  • 30.0 and Above : Obesity


In summary, while BMI provides a quick and straightforward way to assess weight status, it’s essential to recognize its limitations. For personalized health advice, always seek professional guidance. And remember, health is more than just a number—it’s about overall well-being!
